About Tampere

Tampere is the second-largest urban area and third most-populous (over 225 000 habitants) individual municipality in the country, after Helsinki and Espoo municipalities (2014 data). It's also the most populous Finnish city outside the Greater Helsinki area and inner Finland's major urban, economic and cultural hub.

The City of Tampere is running for the title of Europe’s innovation capital in the contest ”The European Capital of Innovation Award – iCapital”, organized by the European Commission. Tampere is growing city with a lot of companies focusing in ICT-development.

Tampere is also home for multiple heavy industry giants like KoneCranes and Kalmar. These companies are pioneers in Internet of BIG things. In addition Tampere based ICT- companies have strong foothold in developing and maintaining national APIs driven data exchange layer (kansallinen palveluväylä) in Finland.
