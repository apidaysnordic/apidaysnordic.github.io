API Days Nordic Conference 2016
18th-19th May, Tampere Hall Congress and Concert Centre, Tampere, Finland

Heavy Industry and APIs & Government Driven API Platforms &

API economy & APIOps - containers, testing, devops, micro services and monitoring

In the recent years there has been a major focus on APIs, how are they deployed and managed. APIs started to play a much more dominant role in the areas of IoT moving beyond merely from enabling any product or service to talk to other products or services. Developers taking a lead role in defining how they want and API to be. In a nutshell APIs have become the indication of the maturity of an organizations IT services.

Join discussion in Facebook group API Days Nordic

API Days Nordic is the first of its kind in Scandinavia with the vibrant participants from Scandinavia and Baltics. Scandinavian & Baltic countries have been pioneering open data and eSystems at the government and city level. For example program like 6Aika and Estonian eResidency. Its a very unique opportunity to meet the teams, organizations and people who make this happen.

In a nutshell APIs have become the indication of the maturity of an organizations IT services and developers are new rockstars.

For whom this event is

  • Organization is planning to create or has already created APIs for sharing service
  • Developers who is using APIs in your development or you are curious about the best practices about designing and managing APIs
  • Curious to know more about terms like "API Economy" or "API Design" or "API Documentation".

API Days Nordic is part of global series of API Days events organized around the world. Two days of exciting, informative and showcases and workshops. Its the event to know how APIs enabled architecture the countries IT, how APIs are playing significant role in connecting heavy machinery, how APIs are playing role in IoT and how APIs have become a back bone open data.

Event is organized by API:Suomi (Finnish API community) with support of API Days global network, COSS (the Finnish Centre for Open Systems and Solutions), API Duo OÜ (API strategy consulting), Tredea (Tampere Region Economic Development Agency) and apinf.io (open source API management). API Days Nordic is 2-day event and will be organized in Tampere, Finland.

The City of Tampere is running for the title of Europe’s innovation capital in the contest ”The European Capital of Innovation Award – iCapital”, organized by the European Commission. Tampere is growing city with a lot of companies focusing in ICT-development. Tampere is also home for multiple heavy industry giants like KoneCranes and Kalmar. These companies are pioneers in Internet of BIG things. In addition Tampere based ICT- companies have strong foothold in developing and maintaining national APIs driven data exchange layer (kansallinen palveluväylä) in Finland.

Our themes for ‘16

API Days Nordic is part of global series of API Days events organized around the world. For the first time in history Scandinavian API community is gathered in one event to share knowledge, good practices, success stories and insights of emergent API economy.

  • Strategy Stream: business imperatives and opportunities afforded by APIs
  • Execution Stream: APIs & DevOps (APIOps), design, architecture, security, quality and new technologies

In addition to above tracks, we have reserved room for community driven sessions.
